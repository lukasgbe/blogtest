We Are Heading In A Wrong Direction

written by Lukas Gruber

Nothing described in the following text should be viewed as financial advice. I recommend doing your own research and keeping up with blockchain news. Nothing described here is stamped as absolutely correct. If you notice something wrong, explain your point in an email.

The blockchain world has evolved quite a bit in recent years. We've gone from simple smart contracts on development environments like Ethereum to Layer 2 solutions that solve impossible problems with seeming ease. We have taken the initial goal of using blockchain as a solution to the corrupt banking system to a level where blockchain is also a solution for dozens of areas, such as supply chain, data storage, and identity recognition.
